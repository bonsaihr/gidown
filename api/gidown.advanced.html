class gidown.advanced.Time[source]¶

Bases: object

Collection of image upload time restrictions that can be used in advanced search options.

static past(amount: int, unit='h')[source]¶

Restrict search to images that were uploaded in the past <amount> <unit>.

To get results from the past 10 weeks you would add the restriction: >>> from gidown.advanced import Time >>> Time.past(10, “w”) tbs=qdr:10w

Allowed units of time:

  • n - minute
  • h - hour
  • d - day
  • w - week
  • m - month
Parameters:
  • amount – Amount of time to look into the past, measured <unit>.
  • unit – One of the allowed units of time. Defaults to hour (h).
Returns:

QuerySetting representing desired restriction.
